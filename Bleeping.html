Bleeping Spaceships MR

A downloadable game

Available at Meta

Bleeping Spaceships MR is a mixed reality bullet-hell arcade game where your living room transforms into an explosive battlefield!

Game Features:

  • ⚡ Mixed Reality or VR Modes
  • 🎮 Intuitive Motion Controls
  • 🌌 32 Handcrafted Stages
  • 🔥 5 Play Modes
  • 👾 4 Epic Bosses
  • 💥 27 Distinct Enemies
  • 🚀 4 Upgradeable Secondary Weapons
  • 🏆 Leaderboards and Stage Ranks
