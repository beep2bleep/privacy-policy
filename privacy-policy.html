Privacy Policy for Bleeping Spaceships MR

Effective Date: June 5, 2025

Bleeping Spaceships MR does not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information from users directly. However, the app may access and use limited user data as required by Oculus/Meta Platform Features. This includes:

  • User ID or Oculus Username – to enable platform-integrated features such as leaderboards or cloud saves
  • User Interaction Data – including in-game progress or scores, strictly for app functionality

This data is processed only within the Meta/Oculus platform and is not transmitted to external servers. The purpose of this processing is to support essential features of the application.

Data Use Purposes

  • To enable in-game features like saving progress or leaderboards
  • To maintain functionality across gameplay sessions
  • To support Oculus/Meta platform services

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. All data processing remains within Meta/Oculus systems where required for app operation.

Data Deletion Requests

If you would like to request deletion of any data associated with your use of Bleeping Spaceships MR, please contact us at web@beep2bleep.com. We will assist with coordinating the deletion process via Meta’s available platform tools.

For more information on Meta’s privacy practices, please visit the Meta Privacy Policy.
